About Me!

I am a 14 year old from The Knowledge Society passionate about emerging technologies and how they can change the world. Some of them include VR/AR, AI, and BCIs. I love tech and have been learning programming since the age of 10. With emerging technologies I want to impact billions, and to do that I am learning about new powerful technologies.

I also have 3 central values most important to me that I want to share:

1. I'm stoic.
I'm stoic and don't let emotion get in my way. I think about actions as rationally as possible and in my work I try not to think emotionally. However it does not mean that I am not feeling emotion, it's good to be thinking that way in a casual conversation for example, but I make important decisions rationally.

2. I figure things out.
By figuring things out, I get to a level of learning I might not get from asking for help. It builds my ability to solve novel problems and the more I practice this, the more I build that ability. Going deep into a topic and just learning about it online by myself is one great way for me to build this mindset as well as learn!

3. I'm curious.
By having curiosity it allows me to find things I am interested in and build a "T". Building a T means that you have knowledge in many subjects from that curiosity, and things you found interesting, you can go deeper into. Curiosity is probably my most important value because without it I wouldn't have learnt so much about all these emerging technologies.
